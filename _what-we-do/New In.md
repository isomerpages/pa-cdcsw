<p><strong>About CDC Vouchers Scheme 2026 (January)</strong>
</p>
<p>As announced in Budget 2025, all Singaporean households will receive an
additional $800 in CDC Vouchers to help with daily expenses. The vouchers
are distributed in two tranches — $500 in May 2025 and $300 in January
2026.</p>
<p>This is in addition to the $300 CDC Vouchers given on 3 January 2025.
Therefore, all Singaporean households will receive a total of $800 CDC
Vouchers in 2025.</p>
<p>Starting 13 May 2025, you can visit&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/cdcv" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">go.gov.sg/cdcv</a>&nbsp;to claim the $500
CDC Vouchers (May). The process for claiming and spending the vouchers
remains the same.</p>
<p>The CDC Vouchers Scheme 2025 (January) and CDC Vouchers Scheme 2025 (May)
are valid till 31 December 2025.</p>
<p><a href="https://vouchers.cdc.gov.sg/residents/info/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Learn</a>&nbsp;how
you can claim your CDC Vouchers 2026 (January).</p>
