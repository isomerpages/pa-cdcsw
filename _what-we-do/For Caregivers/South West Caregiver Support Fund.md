<p>The <strong>South West Caregiver Support Fund</strong> is an interim one-time
assistance of $800, to help caregivers with self-care and caregiving expenses.</p>
<p>The eligibility criteria and administration of the fund is as follows:</p>
<p>a)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Resident of South West District;</p>
<p>b)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Singapore Citizen or at least one immediate
family member is a Singapore Citizen;</p>
<p>c)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Per Capita Household Income (PCHI) or $1,500
and below or hold a Blue CHAS Card;</p>
<p>d)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Caregiver and Care Recipient must reside in
the same household; and</p>
<p>e)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Care Recipient requires assistance in at least
three Activities of Daily Living (ADL), i.e. washing, feeding, dressing,
toileting, moving and transferring; or</p>
<p>f)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Care Recipient certified to have a permanent
disability of any one of the following: Physical / Visual / Hearing / Intellectual
Disability - autism spectrum disorder, etc.</p>

<p>Visit your nearest Community Club/Centre to be referred.</p>
<p>Applications are accepted throughout the year.</p>
