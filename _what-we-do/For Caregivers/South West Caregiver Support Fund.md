<p>The&nbsp;<strong>South West Caregiver Support Fund</strong>&nbsp;is an
interim one-time assistance of $800, to help caregivers with self-care
and caregiving expenses.</p>
<p>&nbsp;The eligibility criteria and administration of the fund is as follows:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Resident of South West District;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applicant or immediate family member is a Singapore Citizen;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Per Capita Household Income (PCHI) of $1,500 and below;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Caregiver and Care Recipient must reside in the same household;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Care Recipient requires assistance with min. 3 of the Activities of Daily
Living (ADL)</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Washing;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Feeding;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Dressing;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Toileting;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Moving and;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Transferring</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><u>or</u></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Care Recipient certified to have a permanent disability of any one of
the following:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Physical</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Visual</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hearing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Intellectual</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Autism</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Others</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>How to Apply</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Visit your nearest Community Club/Centre</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Complete the South West Caregiver Support Fund Referral Form with supporting
documents</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Applications are accepted throughout the year</p>
