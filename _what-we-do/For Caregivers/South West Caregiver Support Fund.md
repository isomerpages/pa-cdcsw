<p>The <strong>South West Caregiver Support Fund</strong> is an interim one-time
assistance of $800, to help caregivers with self-care and caregiving expenses.</p>
<p>&nbsp;The eligibility criteria and administration of the fund is as follows:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Resident of South West District;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Singapore Citizen or at least one immediate family member is a Singapore
Citizen;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Per Capita Household Income (PCHI) or $1,500 and below or hold a Blue
CHAS Card;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Caregiver and Care Recipient must reside in the same household; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Care Recipient requires assistance in at least three Activities of Daily
Living (ADL), i.e. washing, feeding, dressing, toileting, moving and transferring;
or</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Care Recipient certified to have a permanent disability of any one of
the following: Physical / Visual / Hearing / Intellectual Disability -
autism spectrum disorder, etc.</p>
</li>
</ol>
