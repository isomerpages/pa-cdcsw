<p><strong>Green Spaces @ South West</strong>
</p>
<p>Growing an inclusive, caring and active community by transforming community
gardens into green spaces in our district. These pockets of greenery dot
the community and neighbourhood, helping to cool our environment, enhance
our health and nurture an appreciation for nature among the young. Through
this Green Spaces @ South West, we also hope to encourage our residents
to adopt a clean and green lifestyle and to take responsibility for their
living environment.</p>
<p>We provide fundings to GROs, SSAs, RNs and schools for your green spaces.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Seed Funding: For new community gardens, approved by TownCouncil and NParks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Activity Grant: For conducting gardening activities for the community</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support Fund: For existing community gardens, at least 5 years old and
above</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Expansion Fund: For expanded community gardens</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Seed Funding</strong>
</p>
<p>A one-time seed funding for newly formed gardens.</p>
<p><strong>Activity Grant</strong>
</p>
<p>A grant of up to $500 will be provided to community gardens for outreach
of minimally 30 participants per activity. Activities conducted should
be gardening related and benefit the South West community.</p>
<p><strong>Support Fund</strong>
</p>
<p>One-time funding to support the maintenance of community gardens. Funds
must be used for maintenance and upkeep of the garden. Applicable to community
gardens that have applied for Seed Funding at least 5 or more years ago.</p>
<p>Expansion Fund</p>
<p>One-time funding to support garden expansion of existing community gardens.
Expansion of the community garden must be at least half of the size of
the existing garden.</p>
